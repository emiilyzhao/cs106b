Q1:

_id is constant, while _x and _y change by _vy and _vx. Depending on the last
wall that the ball hits, _x += _vx or _x -= _vx and _y += _vy or _y -= _vy.
If the last horizontal wall hit was the left wall, the value is added, and
if the last vertical wall hit was the bottom wall, the value is added.
The opposite applies for the opposite walls, where the value is subtracted.
